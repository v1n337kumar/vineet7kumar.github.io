Hello!

I am Vineet, a human currenly based in Montreal. I grew up in India and have lived in Lucknow, Thanjavur and Pune. I moved to Montreal to experience an international culture while working towards a master’s degree in computer science at McGill University.

I build software for a living and currently work for INRO. My technical interests include compilers, optimizations for numerical computing systems, and systems programming in general. At INRO, I mainly work on tools for numerical computations and data analytics. At McGill, I worked on the McLab project at Sable lab. I use Vim as my primary text editor.

In my free time, I like to read books, sleep, or spend time with my fiancée.
